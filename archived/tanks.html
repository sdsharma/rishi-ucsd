Introducing Tanks by Project RISHI!!


Project RISHI at UCSD is now selling cute American Apparel tanks to raise money for our organization! We have introduced two new projects (sexual health education camps and disabilities awareness program) in rural Maharashtra, India and could really use your help!


These unique tanks are unisex and have the slogan "Always Keep It Open" with a heart logo in the background consistent with our village's message of acceptance to all kinds of people. If you live in or around UCSD, we'll hand-deliver them to you, otherwise postage is included in the price. If you use PayPal, there's a $1 PayPal service fee.


Email ucsdprojectrishi@gmail.com with your name, quantity, size, and location. All purchases are greatly appreciated!
