President - Shilpa Sharma

Shilpa is a third-year undergraduate at UC San Diego, pursuing a degree in General Biology. She is currently serving as the President of the UCSD chapter and is committed to improving health and social conditions in the village of Anandwan located in Maharashtra, India. Throughout her time with Project RISHI, she hopes to better understand the links between health education, disease prevention and treatment, social stigma, and healthcare sustainability. After graduation, she hopes to explore a career in medicine and healthcare, travel the world, and experience other cultures.
